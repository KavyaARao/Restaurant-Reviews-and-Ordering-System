ABOUT US

FoodieBay is a restaurant search and discovery platform which serves the people all over the country. In addition to restaurant search and discovery, FoodieBay has expanded its offering to Online Ordering (Food Delivery), Take Away Services, Table Reservations ,creating cutting-edge technology to connect restaurant businesses and customers. In addition to these services, FoodieBay is also used by consumers to discover, rate, and review restaurants,as well as create their own personal networks of fellow food enthusiasts for trusted recommendations.
FoodieBay brings food from the best restaurants and desserts to your doorstep. No minimum order.
Using FoodieBay, you can order food & beverages online from restaurants near & around you. We deliver food from your neighborhood local joints, your favorite cafes, luxurious & elite restaurants in your area, and also from chains like Dominos, KFC, Burger King, Pizza Hut, FreshMenu, Mc Donald’s, Subway, Faasos, Cafe Coffee Day, Taco Bell, and more. Exciting bit? We place no minimum order restrictions! Order in as little (or as much) as you’d like. We’ll serve it to you!
